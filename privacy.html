Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SJsmartapp ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of PackSmart (the "App").

Information We Collect

When you install PackSmart, we collect information necessary to provide the service, including your Shopify store URL, shop name, and contact email. We also collect product and order data from your Shopify store solely to provide prepack functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve PackSmart. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage

Prepack configuration data is stored in your Shopify store's metafields and in our secure database. We retain this data for as long as you have PackSmart installed.

Data Sharing

We do not share your store data with third parties except as required by law or as necessary to provide the service (e.g., hosting providers).

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by uninstalling the app or contacting us at support@sjsmartapp.com. Upon uninstallation, we will delete your store data within 30 days.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sjsmartapp.com.
